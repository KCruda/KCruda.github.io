SITeS

Description: CSU Society of Information Technology Students is an organization within the campus which is composed of Active students of Caraga State University studying under the course of Information Technology.

The CSU Society of Information Technology Students is a vibrant and dynamic organization located within the campus of Caraga State University. This group is composed of active and enthusiastic students pursuing degrees in Information Technology. Members of the society are dedicated to fostering a collaborative and supportive environment where they can share knowledge, experiences, and innovative ideas. Through a variety of events, workshops, and projects, the society aims to enhance the academic experience and prepare its members for the ever-evolving tech landscape. By engaging in discussions, networking opportunities, and hands-on learning experiences, the CSU Society of Information Technology Students plays a crucial role in shaping the future leaders of the IT industry while also promoting a strong sense of community among students.

GaMEE

Description: GaMEE is an organization of various different students inside Caraga State University, GaMEE is the type of organization that specialize or focuses on the building friendship through playing games and watching gameplays for their favorite games.

GaMEE is a vibrant organization at Caraga State University that brings together students from diverse backgrounds and disciplines, united by their shared passion for gaming. This dynamic group focuses on fostering friendships and building a strong community through the love of video games and interactive entertainment. Whether it's through engaging in multiplayer sessions, hosting tournaments, or gathering to watch live gameplay of their favorite titles, GaMEE creates an inclusive environment where students can connect and collaborate. Members have the opportunity to explore various genres, from competitive esports to immersive role-playing games, enhancing their skills while forming lasting bonds. The organization also organizes events, workshops, and game nights, allowing participants to share strategies, tips, and experiences, further enriching their gaming journeys. By celebrating the culture of gaming, GaMEE not only promotes social interaction but also encourages teamwork, communication, and creativity among its members.
